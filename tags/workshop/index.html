Weekend of Go

January 23 Gopher Gala Giant Gopher Cake Designed by Renee French Gopher Gala is a distributed 48-hour hackathon for the Go programming language. Register and build something awesome! You can fly solo or form a team of up to four people. To hack from Startup Edmonton, you can also register here. Startup Edmonton will be open the full 48 hours. Things kick off Friday, January 23 at 6pm and go until Sunday at 6pm.
