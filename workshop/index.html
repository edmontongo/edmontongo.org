Go is an open source programming language that enables the production of simple, efficient, and reliable software at scale.

Edmonton Go is a meetup to collaborate, discuss, and learn Go.

Introduction to Go Workshop

William Kennedy On January 24, 2015 we will be hosting a Go Workshop taught by William Kennedy of GoingGo.net, co-author of Go in Action and trainer extraordinaire. What You Will Learn The day will cover the following material: Language and Syntax Arrays, Slices and Maps Methods, Interfaces and Embedding Types Concurrency and Channels Schedule The workshop starts at 9:00 am sharp and ends at 5 pm. Come 30 minutes early if you need help setting up a Go environment.
