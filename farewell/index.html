Edmonton Go

Go is an open source programming language that enables the production of simple, efficient, and reliable software at scale. Edmonton Go is a meetup to collaborate, discuss, and learn Go.

Farewell

13 Jan 2022

The Edmonton Go meetup started in 2014, thanks to the support of Startup Edmonton.

It has been fun, but interest in organizing these meetups has dwindled, especially in light of the pandemic.

Visit Meetup for other online events for Go.

Or visit Dev Edmonton Society for other meetups in Edmonton.

Where

Hosted at Startup Edmonton
Suite 301, 10359 - 104 Street

When

Fourth Monday of each month at 6:30 pm.

How to get there

By transit: From Bay Station walk north on 104th St. to Mercer Warehouse.
By car: Check ParkingPanda for rates on Rogers Place event nights.
